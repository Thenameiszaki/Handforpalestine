Mass Rally in Support of Palestinians

Malaysia has a longstanding history of supporting the Palestinian cause, often demonstrated through mass rallies and public demonstrations. These events serve as platforms for Malaysians to express solidarity with Palestinians and to protest against actions perceived as oppressive. On October 22, 2023, thousands gathered in Kuala Lumpur to show support for Palestinians amid escalating tensions in Gaza. The rally, held at Axiata Arena Stadium, was titled "Malaysia is with Palestine" and featured speeches from political leaders, including Prime Minister Anwar Ibrahim. He condemned the violence in Gaza and emphasized Malaysia's unwavering support for the Palestinian people.In addition to government-organized events, grassroots movements have also played a significant role. For instance, from December 26 to 31, 2024, activists organized a sit-in demonstration called the "Blockade for Palestine" outside the U.S. Embassy in Kuala Lumpur. This six-day protest was a response to the U.S. veto of United Nations resolutions calling for a ceasefire in the ongoing Israeli-Palestinian conflict. Participants braved adverse weather conditions and police obstructions to express their solidarity with the Palestinian people.
