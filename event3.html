"Himpunan 1 Tahun Taufan Al Aqsa" Rally

On October 12, 2024, thousands of Malaysians gathered in Kuala Lumpur for the "Himpunan 1 Tahun Taufan Al Aqsa" rally, marking the first anniversary of the "Al Aqsa Storm" operation initiated by Hamas on October 7, 2023. Participants assembled at the Asy-Syakirin Mosque in Kuala Lumpur City Centre (KLCC) before marching to the United States Embassy on Jalan Tun Abdul Razak.The rally, which drew approximately 2,000 demonstrators, featured speeches from political figures, including PAS president Tan Sri Hadi Awang. He emphasized the importance of Muslim unity against Israeli actions, urging collaboration between opposition and government entities for the Palestinian cause.Participants, some traveling from distant states like Kedah, expressed their solidarity with Palestinians by chanting slogans and carrying pro-Palestine placards. The event underscored Malaysia's ongoing support for Palestine and highlighted the nation's collective call for unity in addressing international conflicts.
