Government Support for Gaza Reconstruction

Malaysia has consistently demonstrated its commitment to supporting the reconstruction of Gaza through various initiatives. Recently, the Malaysian Ministry of Foreign Affairs announced plans to host the fourth session of the Conference on Cooperation among East Asian Countries for Palestinian Development (CEAPAD) in collaboration with Japan. This conference aims to bolster international support and cooperation for sustainable reconstruction efforts in Palestine.Prime Minister Anwar Ibrahim has outlined specific projects as part of Malaysia's contribution to Gaza's rebuilding efforts, including the construction of a school, a hospital, and a mosque. These initiatives are designed to address critical infrastructure needs in the region. In addition to governmental efforts, Malaysian non-governmental organizations (NGOs) have been active in providing aid to Gaza. For instance, Mercy Malaysia has a history of delivering humanitarian assistance in the region, including health services and medical supplies. While Malaysia acknowledges that it cannot single-handedly reconstruct Gaza, it emphasizes the importance of international collaboration. By partnering with Gulf countries and other nations, Malaysia aims to contribute effectively to the rebuilding process.
